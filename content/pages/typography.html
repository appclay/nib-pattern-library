Typography helps create the structure and hierarchy of a page. This helps users read, absorb and comprehend the content. We use Roboto for headings and Open Sans for body and UI elements.

These typefaces were chosen for their pairing, extensive range of weights and similarity to Helvetica.

Titles

Titles have no margins by default. When used within a v2-article class they have standardised margin above/below the title.
